Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or transmitted anywhere. This ensures complete privacy and security of your files.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can also convert between formats during compression for optimal results.

What's the maximum file size limit?

You can compress images up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend using our batch processing feature to handle multiple smaller images efficiently.

Does compression affect image quality?

Our advanced algorithms maintain excellent visual quality while significantly reducing file size. You can control the quality level, and our smart compression typically retains 99%+ visual quality.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Yes! You can select multiple images and compress them all at once. You can also download them individually or as a ZIP file for convenience.

Is this service completely free?

Yes, compressimage.co is completely free to use with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or usage limits. Compress as many images as you need!
